Machine Learning

Course Overview

This course explores the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, focusing on how computers identify patterns and generate predictions. The curriculum emphasizes Python-based algorithms, model interpretation, and high-efficiency workflows within Jupyter environments.

  • Interpreted model outputs to evaluate performance and statistical accuracy.
  • Applied data preprocessing techniques, identifying the critical role of data quality and volume in model success.
  • Understanding the differences beetween supervised learning, unsurpervised learning, and reinforcement learning and selecting the right one for specific tasks.
  • Improved efficiency in Jupyter Notebook.
